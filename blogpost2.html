The Weight of the Phrase “I’m Proud of You.”

I don’t know why, but I often feel a little taken aback when certain people congratulate me with the words, “I’m proud of you.” To me, it sometimes feels ironic because I believe this phrase should be reserved for a certain level of intimacy...

Parents are allowed to say it because they’ve invested so much. Friends can say it because they supported you during hard times. A significant other can say it because they’ve shared your journey.

However, when it comes from a distant person, it can feel hollow — as if they’re praising the result, not the journey. At best, they may feel admiration or envy, but not true pride.

For me, pride is not just a word; it’s recognition of struggle, effort, and connection. 💪
