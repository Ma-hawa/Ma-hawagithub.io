Invictus | Poem

September 2025

Between water and sky, I write, I dream and I am, The Invictus

Out of the sea water that flows beneath my feet, spare my unseeable wounds the sting of the salty ocean. I shall hiss for sure when the pain sears through, but neither shall a tear fall, nor a plea escape, for this pain finds, and shall find, me scarred, but not conquered. Firm I shall stand, with my battered feet deep-rooted into the wet shoal, for I was born and raised to be an undefeatable soul.
