✨ Between water and sky, I write, I dream and I am, The Invictus 🌊☁️

Invictus | Poem

Out of the sea water
that flows beneath my feet,
spare my unseeable wounds
the sting of the salty ocean.

I shall hiss for sure
when the pain sears through,
but neither shall a tear fall,
nor a plea escape,
for this pain
finds, and shall find, me scarred,
but not conquered.

Firm I shall stand,
With my battered feet
deep-rooted into the wet shoal,
for I was born and raised
To be an undefeatable soul.
